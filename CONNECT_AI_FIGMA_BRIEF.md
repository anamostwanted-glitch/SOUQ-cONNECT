# 🤖 Connect AI Marketplace: Official Figma Design Brief
**Prepared by:** Connect AI Core Team (Product Manager, UX Researcher, Growth Hacker, Solution Architect)
**Status:** Ready for High-Fidelity Prototyping

---

## 🎨 1. Design Philosophy (The "Neural Core")
*Goal: Create an interface that feels alive, intelligent, and premium.*

### Visual Identity
*   **Aesthetic Style:** **Neural Glassmorphism.** Use semi-transparent white/off-white panels (opacity 80-90%) with a strong background blur (20px+). 
*   **The Bento Matrix:** Layouts should be built on a modular "Bento" grid. Interactive tiles that can change size based on priority.
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   **Logic Blue (`#3B82F6`):** Primaries for Buyers (Search, Discovery, Actions).
    *   **Trust Teal (`#10B981`):** Primaries for Vendors (Analytics, Success states).
    *   **Authority Amber (`#F59E0B`):** Verification, Trust Badges, High-Value alerts.
*   **Typography:**
    *   **Display:** *Space Grotesk* (Bold/Black) - For a tech-forward look.
    *   **Body:** *Inter* - For maximum readability and UI balance.

## 🛍️ 2. The Customer (Buyer) Journey
*Focus: Speed of finding intent and verifying trust.*

### Key Screens to Design
1.  **Neural Discovery Home:** Massive, glowing search bar at top center. Bento grid below showing "Trending Near You" and "Top Match Suppliers."
2.  **Intent Search Results:** Cards showing product images with a "Match %" badge in a gradient of Blue to Teal.
3.  **Deal Chat:** Real-time chat with a "Deal Pulse Bar" at the top showing transaction safety/trust levels. Floating "Smart Action Chips" (e.g., "Request Real Photo").

### Buyer Copy (Labels)
*   **Search Placeholder:** "Tell AI what you're looking for..." | "أخبر الذكاء الاصطناعي عما تبحث عنه..."
*   **Match Label:** "85% Match to your Intent" | "85% مطابقة لطلبك"
*   **Home Header:** "Find your next need." | "جد احتياجك القادم."

---

## 📈 3. The Vendor (Supplier) Journey
*Focus: Data performance, professional identity, and growth.*

### Key Screens to Design
1.  **Growth Pulse Dashboard:** A mission-control dashboard. Bento tiles for "Total Exposure," "Active Leads," and "Market Demand."
2.  **AI Growth Module:** A dark-glass card titled "Connect AI Growth Strategy" with specific diagnostic headlines and "Sales Hacks."
3.  **Pro Storefront Mode:** The **Standalone View**. Massive cover photo, floating logo, "Authority Level" badges (Seed, Root, Growth, Titan).
4.  **Share Hub Hub:** A slide-up menu with 3 link types (Official, Pro Storefront, Marketing Ads).

### Vendor Copy (Labels)
*   **Stat Tiles:** "Total Exposure" (إجمالي الظهور), "Action Rate" (معدل الإجراءات).
*   **AI Insight Header:** "Connect AI Growth Strategy" | "استراتيجية نمو كونكت AI"
*   **Authority Ranks:** "Seed" (بذرة), "Growth" (نمو), "Titan" (عملاق).

---

## 🛠️ 4. Visual & Technical Constraints
*   **RTL First:** All designs **must** be mirrored for Arabic. Icons like `ArrowRight` must flip to `ArrowLeft`. Text alignment must be pixel-perfect for both languages.
*   **Mobile Haptics:** Visually represent button presses with a slight "sink" (Scale to 0.95).
*   **Standalone Feel:** In "Storefront Mode," strictly hide global platform navigation to give the vendor their own "branded" space.
*   **Micro-interactions:** Skeleton loading states should look like a "Neural Pulse" wave passing through the UI elements.

## 🏁 5. Final Figma Audit Checklist
*Use this to verify the designer's work before approval.*

- [ ] **Bento Logic:** Does every dashboard use a consistent grid system?

- [ ] **Dual-Tone Identity:** Is there a clear visual distinction between Buyer (Blue) and Vendor (Teal)?
- [ ] **Intent-First:** Does the search bar dominate the home screen as the primary interaction point?
- [ ] **Arabic Integrity:** Is the Arabic typography as balanced as the English side?
- [ ] **Trust Integration:** Are the "MVM Verified" and "Authority Levels" clearly visible on profiles?
